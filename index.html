Clarity for Cairo: Public Transit Signage Redesign

A UX/UI Design Project focused on legibility, consistency, and accessibility across the Metro, Monorail, LRT, Bus, and Ferry networks.

1. The Problem: A Megacity's Confusion

Cairo's vast transport network often suffers from inconsistent and visually cluttered signage. This creates significant barriers for both residents and tourists, leading to confusion and inefficient journeys.

Existing signage often lacks consistency in language and visual hierarchy.

2. Our Design Principles

Bilingual Focus (Arabic & Latin)

Prioritizing equally clear presentation of both Arabic and Latin scripts for maximum accessibility.

High Legibility

Using chosen typography and high-contrast color palettes specifically tested for quick recognition in busy environments.

Modular System

Creating a flexible system that can be easily scaled and adapted across all transport modes (Metro, Bus, Tram).
